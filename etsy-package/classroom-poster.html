Teachable Machine for Micro:bit • Classroom Guide

1
🧠

Train Your AI Model

Go to teachablemachine.withgoogle.com. Make an Image or Audio project. Show the computer a few examples of each class — a wave, a fist, a clap — then click Export Model and copy the URL.

2
🔗

Paste the Model URL

Open our app in Chrome. Paste your Teachable Machine URL in the box and click Load Model. Your classes light up on screen — the AI is ready.

3
🎥

Start the Webcam

Click Start Webcam. Allow camera access. The AI now watches you live and shows confidence bars for every class, right there in the browser.

4
📡

Connect Your micro:bit

Power the BBC micro:bit V2 with USB or batteries. Click Connect, pick BBC micro:bit [xxxxx] in the Bluetooth prompt. Tap TEST to make sure the LEDs react.

5
🚀

Start Sending — Watch It React!

Click Start Sending. Every time the AI recognises you, the micro:bit reacts: a happy face, an arrow, a shape. Flip on Talk and the computer says out loud what it sees!
