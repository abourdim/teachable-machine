# Testimonial / review capture kit

A v0 Etsy shop's #1 bottleneck is reviews. This kit gives you a deliberate system for turning buyers into reviewers into public case studies.

## The funnel

```
Buyer purchases → Auto-reply thanks them (day 0)
              ↓
     [3 days after delivery]
              ↓
   "Did it pair on Chromebook?" nudge (uses EN/FR/AR templates in ETSY_LISTING)
              ↓
     [7 days after delivery]
              ↓
   Send the showcase ask → asks for a classroom photo + quote
              ↓
   They reply with a photo + quote → send permission waiver
              ↓
     Waiver signed → repost as social proof (Pinterest, Instagram, landing page)
              ↓
   Send thank-you gift (free sticker pack / next-version upgrade code)
```

Every step below has templates in 3 languages.

## Usage rules

1. **Never edit a quote.** If grammar's rough, ask for a re-phrase — don't fix silently. Teachers share screenshots of what they actually said.
2. **Student faces are legally spicy.** Require a signed waiver (template in each language file) before reposting a photo with identifiable students. Permission to "show a friend" ≠ permission to post.
3. **First-name only.** Default to "Ms. Sarah K., 5th-grade STEM teacher, Austin TX." Full name only if they explicitly opt in on the waiver.
4. **US COPPA boundary.** Under-13 faces shouldn't be published at all without a parent-level waiver. Better to use a wide shot of the LED matrix lit up than a student's face. Reserve the face-on quote-card format for teachers.
5. **Reuse once then retire.** The same quote shouldn't appear across more than ~3 assets. Rotate the bank.

## Reward ladder (what to send when they reply)

| Response type | Reward |
|---|---|
| Basic star review on Etsy | Thank-you message, no extra |
| Star review + photo | Free digital sticker pack (`printables/sticker-sheet.pdf` with their school name baked in) |
| Star review + quote + waiver | Free upgrade coupon for the next major version |
| Full case-study (a photo, a quote, 3 specific wins) | Offer to co-bylined blog post + permanent slot on press-kit.html |

Budget: sub-$5 per high-quality testimonial. The ratio-of-return on a good classroom photo beats most paid ads.
