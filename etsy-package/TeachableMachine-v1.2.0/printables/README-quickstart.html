Thank you for buying Teachable Machine for Micro:bit! You just grabbed a zero-install, zero-account, browser-local ML classifier that bridges Google Teachable Machine to a micro:bit V2 over BLE UART. Everything runs on your machine — no cloud after model load, no telemetry, no account. This page walks you through the first three things to do.

First 3 Things To Do

1
Flash the firmware. Open makecode.microbit.org, switch to JavaScript, paste makecode.ts, click Download, and drag the .hex to your micro:bit V2. The board will show an X.
2
Open the app. Double-click index.html in Chrome or Edge. It loads locally. Click Connect, pick your board, watch the status dot turn green.
3
Play. Train a model on teachablemachine.withgoogle.com, copy the URL, paste it in the app. Show a gesture — the label fires. Pair a micro:bit V2 to light up 9 label→LED icons.
