Teachable Machine

Teach a Model, Drive a micro:bit With Your Body
No install • No account • 100% local • EN / FR / AR
1
🤖

Flash the Firmware

Open makecode.microbit.org, switch to JavaScript, paste makecode.ts, hit Download and drag to your micro:bit V2.

2
🌐

Open the App

Double-click index.html in Chrome or Edge. No server, no install. Works offline as a PWA.

3
📡

Pair over BLE

Click Connect, pick BBC micro:bit [xxxxx] in the browser prompt. The board LEDs flash a tick.

4
🎛️

Play with the Tabs

Explore the product features below. Each tab opens a different tool. Press number keys to jump between them.

5
📈

Wrap Up

Try the product in the live demo. Record or save your session to share.
