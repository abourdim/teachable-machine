# 🤖 Teachable Machine → Micro-Bit
### Control a Micro-Bit with your body, face, or sounds!

Welcome! 👋  
In this project, **you teach a computer** to recognize gestures or sounds,  
and the computer **talks to a Micro-Bit** using Bluetooth.

This project is made **for kids, beginners, and classrooms** 💚

---

## 🌟 What can this do?
- ✋ Raise your hand → Micro-Bit reacts  
- 👈 Move left / 👉 move right → LEDs change  
- 👏 Clap → Micro-Bit receives a command  
- 🧠 Learn how **AI** works in a fun way!

---

## 🧰 What you need
- 💻 A computer (Windows / Mac / Linux)
- 🌐 Google Chrome browser
- 📷 A webcam (built-in is fine)
- 🔵 One BBC **Micro-Bit**
- 🔋 USB cable or battery pack

## 🚀 STEP 1 — Train your AI model
1. Go to Teachable Machine
2. Choose **Image** or **Audio** project
3. Create classes like:
   - left
   - right
   - up
   - down
4. Add examples
5. Click **Train Model**
6. Copy the **model URL**

---

## 🌐 STEP 2 — Open the app
1. Download the project ZIP
2. Unzip it
3. Double-click **index.html**

---

## 🧠 STEP 3 — Load your model
1. Paste the model URL
2. Click **Load model**
3. Wait for READY

---

## 📷 STEP 4 — Start webcam
1. Click **Start webcam**
2. Allow camera access

---

## 🔵 STEP 5 — Connect the Micro-Bit
1. Plug in your Micro-Bit
2. Click **Connect**
3. Choose **BBC Micro-Bit**
4. Status shows Connected

---

## 🚀 STEP 6 — Start sending
1. Click **Start sending**
2. Do your gesture or sound
3. Micro-Bit reacts live ⚡

---

## 🧪 Test button
Use **Send TEST** to check Bluetooth without camera.

---

## 🗣 Talking computer
Enable speech to hear what the AI sees.
